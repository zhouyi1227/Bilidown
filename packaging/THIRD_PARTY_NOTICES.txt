The Windows desktop bundle keeps the verified BtbN FFmpeg files as separate,
unmodified executables. The macOS desktop builds compile separate FFmpeg and
ffprobe executables from FFmpeg 8.1.2 with LAME 3.100 enabled for MP3 output.
LAME is distributed under the LGPL; its source is available from:
https://sourceforge.net/projects/lame/files/lame/3.100/

Each build records its source, architecture, configuration, and checksums in
FFMPEG_SOURCE.txt and BUILD_INFO.txt. GitHub Releases also include the exact
source archives used by the desktop builds. Anyone redistributing a desktop
build remains responsible for carrying forward the licenses, notices, build
information, and corresponding-source offer.
